The A.D.D.Y…G Foundation: Providing Experiences in the ARTS for Pediatric Cancer Patients
Addison Glines was a phenomenal actor and a great writer, but most importantly he was a genuinely good person. His love of people and the arts transformed into the A.D.D.Y. G. foundation, which gives terminally ill children the chance to experience the arts. For more info please check out their website at http://www.addygfoundation.com/ or click on their logo above.
