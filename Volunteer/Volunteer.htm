The Kings Players invite volunteers to participate in every area of production, theatre support & maintenance. Basic instruction and guidance is offered to every volunteer. Backstage activities are offered at all skill levels and include: directing, stage managing, sewing, painting, carpentry, prop management, costume design, sound and lighting board operation. Front of house activities include: box office workers, house managers and hosts. Volunteers are also needed to serve on the board of directors, various committees and to assist with marketing and fundraising.

On stage, we are always looking for talent of any type: actors, singers, dancers, you name it. Anyone interested in volunteering at the Temple Theatre can call 584-7241 and leave a message describing your interests and we will make sure the right person gets back to you! Or visit a board meeting, held the 3rd Monday of each month at 6:30pm at the theatre! 514 E. Visalia St. Hanford.

The Kings Players welcomes all volunteer involvement and is a volunteer-driven community theatre.
