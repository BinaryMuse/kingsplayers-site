Sorry...No Sidedoor Productions in work, but check back often!
Sidedoor Productions are plays that for one reason or other fall outside our normal season. Sometimes they are shows designed to showcase new talent or a new director. Sometimes they are shows that just somehow missed the regular season. Or it could be a show done at a remote location or in conjunction with a service group or charity. Whatever the case, Sidedoor Productions receive the same attention as all other Kings Players shows!
