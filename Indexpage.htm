The Kings Players theatre group was established in 1963 by Audrey Leibold with the idea of bringing quality live theatre to Hanford, California. Since then many dedicated people have added their voices to the echoes of our stage. In 1964 the Players moved from the Hanford High auditorium to what was to be their home for over 40 years, the Temple Theatre. We invite you to browse our site, attend a show or volunteer your talents!

You Can't Take It With You

If the Sycamore family is crazy, then the rest of the world is crazier. This is a feel-good, whacky comedy with lots of heart and has been a sure-fire success for the last 70-plus years. It’s a true winner in every sense of the word.

Butterflies Are Free

Young Don Baker has been blind since birth. With his over protective mother following his every move, Don finally decides to take his own apartment in Manhattan and pursue his song writing ambitions. When Don’s mother meets his sexy actress neighbor, her controlling instincts go into overdrive with hilariously touching results.

Show Dates: May 7, 8,14, 15, 16, 21, 22, 23, 28, 29, & 30

The Mousetrap

A group of strangers, one of whom is a murderer, are stranded in a boarding house during a snow storm. It’s another superbly intriguing tale from the foremost mystery writer of her time. A classic Agatha Christie.

Rehearsal For Murder

This is a thrilling "theatrical" mystery in which the theatre becomes the "set" for the play. Everyone connected with this play was involved with another play by the same playwright. At its opening night, exactly one year ago and in this same theatre, the beautiful leading lady, who was also the playwright’s fiancee, was murdered. Watch the startling connections to the murder begin to unfold.

Show Dates: November: 19, 20, 26, 27, 28, December 3, 4, 5, 10, 11, & 12
